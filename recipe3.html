Zucchini Cupcake

Zucchini cupcakes are a delightful and moist dessert option that incorporates shredded zucchini into the batter. This addition not only adds a subtle vegetable flavor but also contributes to the cupcakes' moist texture. Typically spiced with cinnamon and nutmeg, these cupcakes offer a unique twist on traditional cupcakes. Zucchini cupcakes are often topped with cream cheese frosting or a dusting of powdered sugar for added sweetness. This creative treat allows for a delicious and unexpected way to enjoy the goodness of zucchini in a dessert form.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1-1/3 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1-1/2 cups shredded zucchini
